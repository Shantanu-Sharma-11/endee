Galgotias University – Placements, Incubation, and Student Achievements
========================================================================

Placement Overview
------------------
Galgotias University has one of the best placement records among private universities in India. The university's dedicated Training and Placement Cell works year-round to connect students with top recruiters from various industries.

Key Placement Statistics:
- 1200+ organizations have recruited from Galgotias University
- 60 LPA highest package offered (Year 2026)
- 5.4 LPA average package across all programs
- 300+ niche offers in specialized roles
- 100+ startups founded by Galgotias alumni and incubated on campus

Top Recruiting Companies:
Major companies that recruit from Galgotias University include leading names from IT, consulting, finance, manufacturing, healthcare, and e-commerce sectors. The university maintains strong corporate relationships to ensure diverse and high-quality placement opportunities for students across all schools and departments.

The Training and Placement Cell provides students with:
- Pre-placement training and skill development programs
- Resume building and interview preparation workshops
- Aptitude and technical skill enhancement sessions
- Industry-specific certification courses
- Soft skills and communication training
- Mock interviews and group discussion practice

Galgotias Incubation Centre (GIC RISE)
---------------------------------------
The Galgotias Incubation Centre for Research Innovation Startup & Entrepreneurs (GIC RISE) was established in 2021 as a premier hardware-focused incubation centre with a sector-agnostic approach. Its mission is to cultivate a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship nationwide.

GIC RISE Infrastructure:
- Co-working spaces for student startups
- Advanced computer facilities
- FAB Lab for hardware prototyping
- Clinical Research Lab
- Graphics Lab for design and visualization
- EV (Electric Vehicle) Lab
- 3D Printing facilities

Centres of Excellence at GIC RISE:
- Centre of Excellence in Technology and Artificial Intelligence (AI)
- Centre of Excellence in Law, Intellectual Property Law (IP), and Innovation
- Intel Intelligent Systems Centre of Excellence (CoE) HPC
- Centre of Excellence in Artificial Intelligence and Edge Computing
- Centre of Excellence in Blockchain Technology

Services Provided by GIC RISE:
- Pre-incubation support and business idea validation
- MoU signing with the incubator
- Office space, internet, and infrastructure facilities
- Product development and improvement support
- Training, networking, and mentorship from industry experts
- Connection with Venture Capitalists and Angel Investors
- Loan assistance from banks, seed money support
- Legal and IPR (Intellectual Property Rights) support
- Product marketing and commercialization support
- Technology exhibitions and awareness camps
- Entrepreneurial development planning
- Company registration process assistance
- Post-graduation support from the incubator

GIC RISE has incubated 100+ startups and continues to support young innovators in scaling their ventures. For more information, visit: https://gicrise.in/

Student Outstanding Achievements
---------------------------------
Students of Galgotias University have made their mark in academics, research, sports, and entrepreneurship, contributing to a better world. Notable achievements include:

iOS Development Centre:
Galgotias University has an iOS Development Centre where students build and publish apps on the Apple App Store. Recent apps developed by students include:

- VedIQ App – A knowledge and note-taking application launched on the App Store from Galgotias University's iOS Development Centre. The app helps students convert their notes into actionable knowledge.

- Sakhi – Menstrual Companion App – A health and wellness application launched on the Apple App Store that serves as a menstrual cycle tracker and companion for women.

- Artistica App – A creative art and drawing application developed by students and launched on the App Store, enabling users to express their creativity digitally.

- Kipp App – A modern note-taking application reimagining how students organize and access their study notes, launched on the Apple App Store.

Hackathon Participation:
Galgotias University students actively participate in Smart India Hackathon (SIH) 2023 and 2024 editions, with multiple teams qualifying and winning at national level. The university has been a nodal centre for SIH and hosts hackathon events regularly.

Research and Publications
--------------------------
Galgotias University faculty and students actively contribute to research with publications in reputed national and international journals. The university encourages interdisciplinary research and maintains collaborations with educational institutions and research organizations across the globe.

The university hosts the student-run "Galgotias News Network (GNN)" on YouTube, which covers campus events, student achievements, research highlights, and interviews with faculty and industry professionals.
