Galgotias University – Overview and Campus Information
======================================================

About Galgotias University
--------------------------
Galgotias University is one of India's leading private universities, located in Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh. Established under the Uttar Pradesh Galgotias University Act, it is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). The university is situated at Plot No. 2, Sector 17-A, Yamuna Expressway, opposite the Buddha International Circuit, Greater Noida, Gautam Buddh Nagar, Uttar Pradesh 203201.

Galgotias University is known for its academic excellence, industry-focused curriculum, and strong placement records. It is a top-ranked private university in the Delhi NCR region and attracts students from across India and over 80 countries worldwide.

Accreditations and Rankings
---------------------------
Galgotias University is accredited and recognized by several apex bodies:

- University Grants Commission (UGC) – recognized under section 2(f)
- All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) – approved programs
- Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business (AACSB) – international accreditation for business programs
- Uttar Pradesh State Government – established by state act

The university holds a strong position in various national and international rankings and has received multiple awards for academic excellence, innovation, and research.

Campus Infrastructure and Facilities
-------------------------------------
The Galgotias University campus is spread across a vast area on the Yamuna Expressway and features world-class infrastructure:

- Multidisciplinary Laboratories: State-of-the-art laboratories equipped with the latest technology for practical learning across all disciplines. These include FAB Lab, Clinical Research Lab, Graphics Lab, EV Lab, and 3D Printing facilities.
- Central Library: A well-stocked library with thousands of books, journals, e-resources, and digital learning platforms.
- Smart Classrooms: Technology-enabled classrooms with audio-visual aids and interactive learning systems.
- Wi-Fi Campus: The entire campus is Wi-Fi enabled, providing seamless internet connectivity to students and staff.
- Hostels: Separate hostel facilities for boys and girls with modern amenities, security, and dining services.
- Cafeteria and Food Courts: Multiple dining options serving a variety of cuisines to cater to students from diverse backgrounds.
- Medical Centre: An on-campus medical facility for emergency health services and routine check-ups.
- Auditorium and Convention Centre: Large auditoriums for events, seminars, guest lectures, and cultural programs.
- Transportation: University bus services connecting the campus to major locations in Greater Noida and the NCR region.
