Galgotias University – Policies, Admissions, and Student Services
==================================================================

Admission Process
-----------------
Galgotias University offers admissions to undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma, and doctoral (PhD) programs across all its schools and departments. The admission process includes:

- Online application through the university's admission portal
- Entrance examination or merit-based selection depending on the program
- Counseling and seat allotment
- Document verification and fee payment

For international students, the university has a dedicated International Office that provides:
- Guidance on admission eligibility and process
- Preparatory English Foundation Program for language support
- Foundation and Bridge Courses for academic readiness
- Visa and FRRO support
- Pre and post-arrival information and assistance
- International Students Grievances Wing for addressing concerns
- Clubs specifically for international students
- International student programs for cultural integration

Galgotias University attracts students from over 80 countries, and the International Office ensures a smooth transition and comfortable experience for all international students.

Fee Structure and Financial Support
------------------------------------
Galgotias University offers transparent fee structures for all programs. Fee payments can be made through the online payment portal. The university also provides:

- Education loan assistance in collaboration with leading banks
- Scholarship programs for meritorious students
- Bihar Student Credit Card Scheme support
- Financial counseling and guidance

Scholarships
Galgotias University offers various scholarships based on:
- Academic merit and entrance exam performance
- Sports achievements and extracurricular excellence
- Financial need and economic background
- Special scholarships for students from specific states or regions

Examination Policies
--------------------
Galgotias University follows a semester-based examination system:

- Mid-Term Examination (MTE): Conducted during the middle of each semester to assess ongoing learning progress.
- End-Term Examination (ETE/SEE): Semester End Examinations are conducted at the end of each semester covering the complete syllabus.
- Practical Examinations and Viva: Conducted for laboratory courses and project-based assessments.
- Backlog and Mercy Examinations: Students who do not clear regular exams can appear for backlog or mercy examinations conducted at scheduled dates.
- Debarred Examinations: Students who are debarred from regular exams due to attendance or other reasons can take debarred examinations as per university notifications.

Examination datesheets, notifications, and results are published on the university portal. Students can access their exam schedules and results through the Student Login portal.

Grading System:
The university follows a grading system that includes grades such as A, B, C, D, F (Fail), AB (Absent), DB (Debarred), and DBE (Debarred End-Term). Students must maintain a minimum GPA to continue their enrollment.

Attendance Policy
-----------------
- Students are required to maintain a minimum of 75% attendance in each course to be eligible for appearing in the End-Term Examination.
- Students falling below the required attendance threshold may be debarred from the examination.
- Attendance is tracked through the university's digital system, and students can check their attendance records through the Student Login portal.
- Special provisions may be made for students with genuine medical emergencies or participation in university-approved events.

Student Services and Support
-----------------------------

Student Login Portal:
Students can access their academic records, attendance, examination details, fee payment, and other services through the online Student Login portal at gustudent.icloudems.com.

Learning Management System (LMS):
Galgotias University provides a comprehensive Learning Management System (LMS) for digital learning, available at lms.galgotiasuniversity.org. The LMS hosts course materials, assignments, lecture recordings, and interactive learning resources.

Digital Learning:
The university supports digital learning initiatives and is a participant in the SWAYAM platform for online courses.

Counselling Services:
The university provides counselling services to support students' mental health and well-being, including a dedicated counselling cell.

National Academic Depository (NAD):
Galgotias University is connected to the National Academic Depository for digital verification and storage of academic certificates and degrees.
